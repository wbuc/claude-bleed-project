PRIVACY AT A GLANCE

Last updated: 26 February 2026

Your data stays on your device. Here's what that means.

**What we store on our servers:**
- Your email address (to keep your account working)
- Donation amount and date (if you choose to donate)

**What we don't store on our servers:**
- Your cycle data, health notes, or personal logs
- Your location, contacts, or photos
- No analytics, no cookies, no ad tracking

**Your health data is yours alone.** Everything you log is stored on your phone and never sent to us. We can't see it and we can't access it.

**The app works offline.** You only need internet to sign in or register.

**Back up your own data.** Go to Settings and export your data as a JSON file. If your phone is lost or reset, we can't recover your data for you. Upload that file to restore it.

**Authentication.** You sign in with email, Google, or Apple. Those providers handle sign-in under their own privacy policies.

**Your rights.** You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of the data we hold (email and donation records). Contact info@mybleed.io and we'll respond within one month.

**Deleting your account.** Go to Settings and tap Delete Account. This wipes all data from your phone and deletes your account from our servers. You can also email info@mybleed.io to request deletion.

Read the full privacy policy at [https://mybleed.io/privacy-policy](https://mybleed.io/privacy)

Questions? Contact info@mybleed.io
